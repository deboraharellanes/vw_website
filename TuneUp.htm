Getting Started

A VW engine should be tuned at least every 5000 to 10,000 miles. An out of tune engine does not run well, poor gas mileage, and wears out sooner. If the car is driven hard, or in extreme weather conditions, or is modified for high performance, it should be tuned every 2000 to 3000 miles Tune ups and maintenance checks take up to two hours.
