Preparing to Remove the Engine

Two bolts and two studs attach the engine to the transmission.

  1. Disconnect the ground strap from the battery
  2. Disconnect the generator cables
  3. Remove the air cleaner and the engine cover plate
  4. Rotate the distributor
  5. Disconnect the throttle cable
  6. Remove the choke electrical connections, coil, electromagnetic cut-off
  7. Remove the oil pressure sending unit
  8. Disconnect the fuel hose at the front engine cover plate
  9. Remove the oil dipstick
  10. Remove the cooling intake
  11. Disconnect the heater flap cables, and unscrew the two lower engine mounting nuts
  12. Jack the car up
  13. Unscrew the nuts
